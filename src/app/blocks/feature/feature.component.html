Features

Mean of vector magnitude. Sample mean of the VM.
SD of vector magnitude. Standard deviation of VM.
Mean angle of acceleration relative to vertical on the device. Sample mean of the angle between x axis and VM.
SD of the angle of acceleration relative to vertical on the device. Sample standard deviation of the angles.
Dominant frequency of vm. Frequency corresponding to the largest modulus.
Fraction of power in vm at dominant frequency. Modulus of the dominant frequency/sum of moduli at each frequency.
Percentage of the power of the vm that is in 0.6-2.5 Hz. Sum of moduli corresponding to frequency in this range divided by sum of moduli of all frequencies.
Collect location information
